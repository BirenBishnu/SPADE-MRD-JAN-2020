Spade is a Tier-5 Student organisation under the aegis of Division of Student Welfare, Lovely Professional University which is working for the motto "Dig out the hidden talent" and which is "by the student", "for the student" and "to the student" to enhance professional and service aspiration of student through participation in individual and group activities in order to bring forward the hidden talent and provide them an opportunity.
Our mission is not merely to survive, but to thrive; and to dig out the hidden talent and provide the platform with some passion, some compassion, some humor, and some style.
Any student who are interested to enhance their professional skills by organizing events can enroll here and "TRANSFORM YOUR IDEAS INTO REALITY".
